SEVENTEEN's BIOGRAPHY

SEVENTEEN or SVT (세븐틴), is a South Korean boy band formed by Pledis Entertainment in 2015. The group consists of 13 members and it's divided into three sub-units with a different specialization : 'Hip-Hop Unit', 'Vocal Unit', and 'Performance Unit'. Seventeen is considered as a self-producing idol group. The members being actively involved in the songwriting, choreographing, and other aspects of their performance.
